Places to Go

Vietnam is a land of breathtaking natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and unforgettable experiences. Let’s explore some of the must-visit destinations across the country.

Interactive Map

Explore Vietnam by clicking on the map regions below:

Northern Vietnam

  • Hanoi: The charming capital of Vietnam, known for its Old Quarter, Hoan Kiem Lake, and street food culture.
  • Ha Long Bay: A UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its emerald waters and towering limestone karsts.
  • Sapa: A stunning mountain town surrounded by terraced rice fields and home to diverse ethnic minorities.
  • Ninh Binh: Known as the "Ha Long Bay on land," featuring scenic boat rides through caves and lush landscapes.

Central Vietnam

  • Hue: The ancient capital of Vietnam, with the Imperial City and historic pagodas.
  • Da Nang: A vibrant coastal city with the famous Golden Bridge and beautiful beaches.
  • Hoi An: A picturesque town with lantern-lit streets and a rich cultural heritage.
  • Phong Nha-Ke Bang National Park: A UNESCO site with some of the world’s largest caves, including Son Doong.

Southern Vietnam

  • Ho Chi Minh City (Saigon): A bustling metropolis blending modern skyscrapers with French colonial landmarks.
  • Mekong Delta: Known as the “Rice Bowl of Vietnam,” explore floating markets and lush waterways.
  • Phu Quoc Island: A tropical paradise with pristine beaches and luxury resorts.
  • Cu Chi Tunnels: A historic underground network used during the Vietnam War.

Special Destinations

  • UNESCO Heritage Sites: Vietnam is home to eight UNESCO Heritage Sites, including My Son Sanctuary and Hue’s Imperial City.
  • Beach Resorts: Vietnam boasts stunning beaches like Nha Trang, Da Nang, and Mui Ne.
  • Highlands & Mountains: Explore the cool climates and coffee plantations of Dalat.
